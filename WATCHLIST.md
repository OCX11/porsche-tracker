# Günther Watchlist — Porsche Alert Criteria

## Tier System
Cars are classified into two tiers based on model and trim:
- **Tier 1 (GT/Collector)**: GT3, GT2, GT4 (any suffix), Turbo S, Spyder, Speedster, Sport Classic,
  Cayman R, air-cooled 911s (pre-1998: 930, 964, 993 generations), 356, any 718 GT4/GT4 RS
- **Tier 2 (Standard)**: All other 911, Boxster, Cayman, 718 variants

---

## Tier 1 — GT / Collector Alerts

**Alert on ANY new Tier 1 listing at any price:**
- Any GT3, GT3 RS, GT2, GT2 RS, GT4, GT4 RS (996/997/991/992/718)
- Any 930, 964, 993 generation 911 (air-cooled era, pre-1998)
- Any 356, 914-6, or 916
- Any Turbo S (any generation)
- Any Spyder variant (Boxster Spyder 987/981, 918 Spyder)
- Any Speedster or Sport Classic
- Any Cayman R

**Also alert if priced more than 10% BELOW comparable FMV** — these are especially notable.

---

## Tier 2 — Standard Alerts

**Only alert on Standard listings when they are clearly below market:**
- Any Tier 2 listing priced 5% or more below its generation's FMV
- Any Tier 2 listing with a price drop > 5% from the previously recorded price

**Do NOT alert on every new standard listing** — only flag standout deals.

---

## Price Drop Alerts (both tiers)
- Tier 1: any price drop > 3% is notable — alert
- Tier 2: alert only on price drops > 5%

---

## Skip / Do Not Alert
- Cayenne, Macan, Panamera, Taycan (excluded at scrape time)
- High-mileage (>80,000 mi) Tier 2 cars UNLESS they had a significant price drop (>5%)
- Duplicate alerts for the same VIN within 24 hours
